What We Do

Custom Balloon
Decorations/Installations

Creating bespoke balloon arrangements for events like birthdays, weddings, corporate functions, and showers, including unique sculptures and themed decor.

Balloon Arches
and Garlands

Designing and installing classic or organic style arches and flowing garlands for entrances, photo ops, and focal points.

Balloon Bouquets
and Centerpieces

Crafting unique helium or air-filled balloon arrangements for tables, gifts, or special highlights.

Welcome Easels

Designing attractive balloon displays on easels to welcome guests to events, often personalized with names, event titles, or themes.

Photo Backdrops

Constructing eye-catching balloon backdrops specifically designed for memorable photo opportunities.

Event Cart Rentals
(Bloom Bars & Candy Carts)

Providing beautifully styled event carts, perfect for creating interactive bloom bars, delightful candy stations, or other unique guest experiences (often adorned with balloons).
